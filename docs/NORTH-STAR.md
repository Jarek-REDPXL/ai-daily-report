# RedPxl News — North Star (master plan)

> The canonical reference the daily routine and every working session align to.
> Read this before writing reports, cards, prompts, or code. When a brief or change
> conflicts with the STANDARD below, STOP and surface it — don't guess.

## VISION
RedPxl News is the team's external brain for craft — a living, compounding
intelligence that makes us the fastest, sharpest operators in design, development,
and marketing, by a widening margin. It watches the relevant world daily, discards
95%, and surfaces only plays that make us measurably better/faster — each a runnable
recipe with exact tools, prompts, and a real link. Nothing decays; every play is a
living card. It learns from our ratings, our "we ran this and it worked," our
requests, and eventually our real results. It doesn't just tell — it does the next
step. Ask it anything across years of mastery, get a cited answer, wherever we work.

## FINISH LINE (done when ALL hold)
- Every card is a practical run-it-today play with real links.
- All 9 domains produce cards steadily (hubs full, not promises).
- The team rates + contributes and the routine demonstrably adapts.
- It ingests real results and calibrates to what works for us.
- Ask-anything retrieval with cited answers, exposed as an MCP.
- Cards spawn the next action.
- It reaches us where we work with per-person feeds.
- Runs unattended, never regresses, new hires productive day one.

## THE STANDARD (bar for everything)
- A card is a PRACTICAL, AI-POWERED, RUN-IT-TODAY play for a craft — advanced but
  non-technical, step-by-step with exact tools/prompts/clicks and a clear payoff.
- Voice = a sharp operator sharing a trick with a POV, never news/specs/encyclopedia.
- Every card carries >=1 REAL working source/tool link — NO fabrication (can't verify
  a real URL, drop the item).
- Kill 95% — leaner beats padded.
- Single source of truth, no mirrors.
- Additive + gate-validated; UI approval-gated; never break the live site.
- Free/no-build by default; backend only where unavoidable.
- RedPxl brand discipline, banned patterns honored.
